The Gen-AI Study Jam 2024 is a hands-on, immersive learning event focused on Generative AI (Gen-AI), one of the most exciting and rapidly evolving fields in artificial intelligence. Hosted by GDG on Campus IPS Academy Indore , this study jam is designed to provide participants with the foundational knowledge, tools, and resources they need to get started with AI-driven creativity. This event is open to both beginners and those who have some prior experience with AI and are looking to enhance their skills.
